Welcome to Teaching Academy

Who Are We?

We are a group of professional individuals with various IT backgrounds who work in reputed multinational companies, startups, and product-based companies. We aim to fill the gap between the college education system and what IT companies actually need from college students.We fulfill this gap by making students industry-ready and providing them with the required skills to crack a job. Our team of experienced professionals and mentors are dedicated to providing the best learning experience to our students.

Career Counselling

We help you explore the different technologies/domains that exist in the market and guide you to choose the one that intrigues you the most. Our experienced mentors are dedicated to providing the best guidance and support to our students.

Simulation Labs with Real-World Problems

We make sure that all our students understand how actual IT works, what they are expected to do, and how to deal with real-world situations. Our simulation labs are designed to provide students with practical experience in solving real-world problems.Our mentors are experts in their respective fields and have years of experience in solving complex IT problems. They will guide you through the simulation labs and provide you with the necessary support to enhance your learning experience.

Dedicated Mentors

We provide dedicated mentors to guide you through your learning journey and help you solve your doubts. Our mentors are experts in their respective fields and have years of experience in the IT industry.

Who can apply

Students who are highly motivated and curious to learn and try out new things/technologies. Students who can dive deep to find out the root cause of some challenging problem and can think out of the box to get solutions. We don't care about the percentages you got, the degree you are studying, whether you have any backlogs, etc.

Who Are Not Eligible?

Students who are well aware of how IT works.
